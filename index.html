Melanoma Skin Cancer in Australia

Key Observations

Australia ranks among the countries with the highest rates of skin cancer globally, reflecting the impact of its sunny climate and outdoor lifestyle. While melanoma constitutes only 1-2% of all skin cancers, it stands out as the most dangerous type due to its aggressive nature.

Recent years show an increasing trend in melanoma cases, especially among males, indicating a need for heightened awareness and preventive measures.
