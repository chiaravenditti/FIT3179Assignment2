Skin Cancer in Australia

Australia ranks among the countries with the highest rates of skin cancer globally. While melanoma accounts for only 1-2% of all skin cancers, it is the most dangerous type due to its ability to spread rapidly to other areas of the body. This visualization examines melanoma data in Australia to raise awareness of its prevalence and lethality, highlighting that males are more susceptible to this condition and that the incidence of cases is on the rise over the years.
